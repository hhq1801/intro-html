Analytical Onomasticon to the Metamorphoses of Ovid

Summary

An electronic reference work to all devices of language by which persons are referenced in the Metamorphoses of Ovid; a series of indexes allow the user to analyse persons by how they are named and to discover and trace interconnections among them and therefore among the stories in which they appear.
