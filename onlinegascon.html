Rôles Gascons en ligne / Online Gascon Rolls

Project URL

http://gasconrolls.org

Summary

This research project builds on the AHRC-funded ‘Gascon Rolls (1317-1468) project, and will create French-language tools for editing and publishing historical materials relating to the ‘Gascon Rolls’, a key documentary source emanating from the English chancellery, preserved in The (English) National Archives which is an important source of knowledge regarding Medieval Aquitaine, the challenges of long distance government or and the territorial dynamics in Aquitaine during the Hundred Years War. The project aims to explore the historical/technical issues in adapting historical data to wider research frameworks, including the possible connection of data to/from other disciplinary settings), the application of geospatial approaches to historical data, data mining approaches to historical editing/re-editing, new visualisations of historical data and initial exploration of multi-device publishing. A new Leverhulme-funded project continues the research carried out here:

  • Old wine in new bottles: English Gascony (1360-1453) for the Digital Future
