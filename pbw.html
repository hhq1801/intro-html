Prosopography of the Byzantine World

Project URL

http://www.pbw.kcl.ac.uk 

Summary

The project's goal is to record in a computerised relational database all surviving information about every individual mentioned in Byzantine sources during the period from 10-25-1102, and every individual mentioned in non-Byzantine sources during the same period who is 'relevant' (on a generous interpretation) to Byzantine affairs.
