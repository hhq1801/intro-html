Old wine in new bottles: English Gascony (1360-1453) for the Digital Future

Project URL 

http://gasconrolls.org

Summary

This project, which builds on work carried out by the AHRC-funded Gascon Rolls (1317-1468) project, lies at the interface of historical and digital humanities research. It integrates established historical methodology with research into an environment for editing chancery rolls (or other historical source materials) which involves:

  • Developing common editorial schemes (where appropriate)
  • Providing tools accessible not just to well-funded institutions but also to lone scholars and record societies/regional learned societies
  • Allowing scholars to provide publicly accessible opinions on connections between people, places and subjects in their texts
  • Facilitating cross-searching across corpora
  • Allowing alternative readings
  • All within documented, peer-review canonical reference systems
