Impact of Digitised Resources - Arcadia funded research

Project URL

http://www.kdcs.kcl.ac.uk/innovation/impact.html

Summary

This project will construct a synthesis of methodologies and techniques and resolve these into a cohesive and achievable methodology for impact assessment of digitised resources and collections.

The results of this study will be made available through the KDCS and the Arcadia Fund's websites via Open Access.
