Integrating Digital Papyrology 2

Project URL

http://idp.atlantides.org/

Summary

IDP is a collaboration between the Duke Databank of Documentary Papyri (DDbDP), the Heidelberger Gesamtverzeichnis der griechischen Papyrusurkunden Ägyptens (HGV) the Advanced Papyrological Information System (APIS) and several leading research institutions. Our goal is to bring together, through collaboration and interoperation, a growing constellation of digital resources for the study, publication and long-term maintenance of ancient documents preserved on papyrus.
