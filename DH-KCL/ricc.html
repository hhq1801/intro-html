Summary

This was a collaborative project with the Centre for Security Analysis in the School of Social Science and Public Policy, involving the International Atomic Energy Agency in Vienna. The CSA part of the project involved open source information collection and analysis in relation to two international regions - the Middle East and the Far East. The contribution of DDH was to provide advice and help to develop computer-based information structures to support the information collection and analysis, and its dissemination to the IAEA.
