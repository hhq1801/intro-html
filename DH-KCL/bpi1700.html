British Printed Images to 1700 (bpi1700)

Project URL

http://www.britishmuseum.org

Summary

The core of this project will be an online library of over 10,000 printed images from early modern Britain. Most of these will come from the Department of Prints and Drawings at the British Museum. These will be complemented by items from other collections, notably the Victoria and Albert Museum. The outcomes will include a fully searchable database.
