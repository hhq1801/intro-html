Inscriptions of Aphrodisias

Project URL

http://insaph.kcl.ac.uk 

Summary

The Inscriptions of Aphrodisias project brings together two major publications from the ancient Greek city of Aphrodisias. In 2004 the team completed work on the EpiDoc Aphrodisias Pilot project, publishing 250 late antique texts from the site; that project was made possible by the support of the Leverhulme Trust and the British Academy. In 2007 a further 1700 texts were added, providing access to inscriptions from all periods of the ancient city's occupation.
