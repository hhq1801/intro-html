Summary

TACTweb connects TACT to the World Wide Web -- making a TACT TDB database accessible to the entire WWW community. By using WWW forms users get access to some of the interactive services that TACT provides them -- but without requiring them to use TACT itself, or have a copy of your TACT database on their own machine.
