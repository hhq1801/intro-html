Digital Shikshapatri

Project URL

http://www.shikshapatri.org.uk 

Summary

Shikshapatri, a fragile Sanskrit manuscript, was written by Shree Swaminarayan, founder of Swaminarayan Hinduism, and outlines moral and spiritual codes for everyday life. It has been digitised to allow a bigger audience to explore this delicate text and the website offers a wide variety of online resources which set the Shikshapatri in its historical, cultural and religious context.
