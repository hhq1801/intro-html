Inscriptions of Roman Cyrenaica

Project URL

http://ircyr.kcl.ac.uk/

Summary

This Leverhulme Trust-funded project will publish some 2400 Greek and Latin inscriptions from the Roman province of Cyrenaica (roughly the eastern half of the seaboard of modern Libya) as an online corpus. Texts are encoded in TEI XML (EpiDoc); photographs held in Cambridge, Rome and Cyrene are being scanned to illustrate the collection; ancient geographical locations are being linked to places identified by the Pleiades project with a view to creating a digital map of the monuments in Libya.
