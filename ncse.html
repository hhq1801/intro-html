Nineteenth-Century Serials Edition

Project URL

http://www.ncse.ac.uk

Summary

NCSE is a research project in nineteenth-century serials which is creating an electronic edition of a cluster of six titles. In addition to full text and other searches, the edition will include historical and critical apparatus, allowing extensive research within and across titles in the cluster. The edition will feature full-text facsimiles, including adverts, illustrations, supplements, portraits and multiple editions, and provide sophisticated searching and indexing functions.
