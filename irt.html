Summary

This is the republication of a volume of 1000 inscriptions (almost all in Latin) of the Roman period from western Libya: the original volume was published in 1952, but with very little illustration, and very sketchy maps. This re-edition makes no alterations to the academic content (except for the incorporation of printed addenda and corrigenda by the author). The new elements are that it includes photographs of almost all the texts, and it maps the data onto the map of Libya in Google Maps or Google Earth. It also includes new English translations of all the texts.
